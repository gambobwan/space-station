About Academy Cinemas

Located in the heart of Portland, Oregon in the Pearl district.

Our reels have been spinning since 1959. Showing our guests a great time is our passion and seeing their smiles is our pleasure!

Accomodations

All our auditoriums are equipted with brand new christie 3D® sound systems.

Relax in our extra-wide, plush cushioning recliners. Seating is on a first-come first-serve basis. Our seating arrangement is disability friendly.

Our auditoriuns are climate controlled to approximately 72° year-round
